Episode 1 Summary – A Smarter Supplement Strategy

  • Supplements aren’t always necessary — many healthy individuals don’t benefit from them.
  • They become important when stress, medication, ageing, or injury increases nutrient demand or reduces absorption.
  • The right dose depends on your health, genetics, and medications — it’s not one-size-fits-all.
  • Form matters: bisglycinate, citrate, or malate forms of magnesium are better absorbed.
  • Choose well: look for clear labels, reputable brands, and avoid hype or multi-herb blends.
  • If a supplement sounds too good to be true — it usually is.

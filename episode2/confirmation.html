Quick Takeaways – Episode 1: A Smarter Supplement Strategy

  • Supplements aren’t always needed — many healthy people don’t benefit.
  • They become essential when stress, medication, ageing factors, or injury increase nutrient demand or reduce absorption.
  • The correct dose depends on your health, genes, and medications — not a one-size-fits-all.
  • Form matters: magnesium bisglycinate, citrate, or malate are better absorbed.
  • Choose quality: clear labels, trusted brands, no hype or multi-herb blends.
  • If it makes bold promises or sounds too good to be true — it probably is.
